Welcome to Seventh Sky

Seventh Sky Coffee Lounge is being developed as a premium hospitality-driven coffee lounge and community gathering space. Our vision is to create a welcoming environment where professionals, entrepreneurs, students, remote workers, and community members can connect, work, learn, and grow.

Coming Soon: We are currently evaluating location opportunities in Chicago, with Bronzeville as the primary launch market, West Loop as an alternative market under review, and Uptown as a future expansion market.

Founder

Bobby Glover

Founder & Managing Member

Bobby Glover is an experienced business and operations leader with more than 30 years of leadership experience in hospitality, restaurants, hotels, customer service, financial management, and multi-site operations.

His leadership philosophy is simple: take care of your employees, take care of your customers, take care of your obligations, plan for challenges before they occur, and build stability before growth.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that bring people together, inspire personal and professional growth, and create meaningful opportunities for connection within the communities we serve.
